Program Highlights

Maury NJROTC was established to provide students with opportunities to develop leadership skills, learn about naval traditions, and serve the local community. Since its inception, it has been a cornerstone of character development and academic achievement at Maury High School. The program emphasizes discipline, responsibility, and teamwork, preparing cadets with the skills and confidence to excel in their future careers and contribute to society.

Leadership Training: Cadets undergo structured training to build leadership skills, fostering confidence and responsibility through hands-on experiences.

Academic Excellence: Emphasis on strong academic performance ensures that cadets are well-prepared for future personal and professional success.

Community Service: Cadets actively participate in service projects, giving back to the local community and instilling a sense of civic duty and teamwork.
