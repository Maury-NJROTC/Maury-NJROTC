Program Highlights

Maury NJROTC focuses on developing cadets through leadership training, academic excellence, and community service, providing them with the skills and experiences needed to excel personally, academically, and within their communities.

Leadership Training: Cadets undergo structured training to build leadership skills, fostering confidence and responsibility through hands-on experiences.

Academic Excellence: Emphasis on strong academic performance ensures that cadets are well-prepared for future personal and professional success.

Community Service: Cadets actively participate in service projects, giving back to the local community and instilling a sense of civic duty and teamwork.
